Let’s Run Together…

Errunds is an online community marketplace connecting local brick and mortar businesses to community shoppers to provide a better shopping experience and to help community businesses scale. Errunds envisions a day where we can put more community businesses on a map, reinvent the way we interact with them, grow our local economies and in the process create smarter cities. Our mission is to disrupt the way we interact with our community all the while preserving and empowering local businesses.

In just the 9 months of our inception, we have made phenomenal strides in our goals from getting seed funding and getting accepted into incubators to picking up industry mentors and validating our concept. But we also know that we have a long way to go. We’re currently growing our kickass team and are trying to bring on talented individuals who, like us, want to push the needle forward.
